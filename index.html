What I do

Drafting + Design

With expertise in architectural drafting and design, I offer comprehensive solutions tailored to meet your specific needs. Whether you require detailed floor plans, elevation drawings, or construction documentation, I provide precise and meticulous drafting services. I combine my technical proficiency with a keen eye for design aesthetics, ensuring that each project reflects your vision while adhering to industry standards. From residential buildings to commercial spaces, my goal is to deliver functional and visually stunning designs that optimize space utilization and create a seamless flow throughout the built environment.

3D Visualization

Unlock the potential of your designs with immersive and captivating 3D visualizations. Through state-of-the-art technology and meticulous attention to detail, I transform architectural plans into realistic and vibrant representations. From interior renderings that showcase material textures, lighting, and furniture arrangements to exterior perspectives that capture the essence of your project in its context, my 3D visualizations bring your vision to life. Whether you are a developer, architect, or interior designer, these dynamic visuals enable you to communicate your ideas effectively, attract clients, and make informed decisions throughout the design process.

BIM Management

As a skilled BIM (Building Information Modeling) manager, I provide comprehensive support and coordination to streamline your construction projects. By leveraging the power of BIM technology, I facilitate collaboration between various stakeholders, including architects, engineers, and contractors. From conceptual design to construction and facility management, I oversee the entire BIM process, ensuring accurate model creation, clash detection, and data integration. By adopting BIM methodologies, your project benefits from improved efficiency, reduced costs, enhanced coordination, and increased overall project quality. Trust me to optimize your project workflow, minimize errors, and maximize the potential of BIM for your construction projects.
